Service Questions

A Full Valet covers both the inside and outside of your car at a standard level of clean. Interior Detailing is interior-only, but much deeper — it includes shampooing seats and carpets and treating odours, which the Full Valet doesn't include.

We can work with your outdoor tap and a standard plug point if available. If neither is available at your location, let us know when booking — we can bring our own water supply for an additional callout fee.

Our Exterior Polishing service can reduce the appearance of light scratches and swirl marks, but we don't offer panel beating, paintwork repair, or dent removal. For serious bodywork damage, we can recommend a trusted panel shop in your area.
